Frequently Asked Questions


  • WHAT ARE THE COURSES OFFERED BY POORNIMA INSTITUTE OF ENGINERRING & TECHNOLOGY(PIET) ?
    •

    Ans. Poornima Group of Colleges offers U.G. & P.G. courses in Engineering and Management.

  • ARE THE COURSES OFFERED BY THE PIET RECOGNIZED ?
    •

    All courses are Approved by AICTE, Govt. of India and Affiliated to Rajasthan Technical University, Kota

  • WHAT IS THE REAP CODE OF POORNIMA INSTITUTE OF ENGINERRING & TECHNOLOGY?

    • REAP Code - 050

  • IS THE ESTABLISHMENT YEAR OF POORNIMA INSTITUTE OF ENGINERRING & TECHNOLOGY?

    • Poornima College of Engineering (PCE) – 2000
    Poornima Institute of Engineering & Technology (PIET) – 2007
    Poornima Group of Institute (PGI) – 2009.

  • IS THE MODE OF FEE DEPOSITION FOR COLLEGE, HOSTEL & TRANSPORT ?

    • The fees are not accepted by cheque /cash. The Bank Demand draft can be drawn from any nationalized bank in favor of a College Name? payable at Jaipur for all fees (College, Hostel & Transport).

  • I AVAIL LOAN FACILITY FOR COURSES AT PIET ?

    • Yes you can avail loan facility at PIET. We provide complete loan documents & Assistance. Bank application and further processing is to be done on behalf of student part.

  • WHAT ABOUT HOSTELS ACCOMMODATION ?
    •

    We have 9 Boys hostel (for 970 boys) and 6 Girls hostel (for 670 Girls) with option of air-cooled accommodation with twin sharing basis.

  • WHAT ARE THE SCHOLARSHIPS AVAILABLE IN THE PIET ?
    •

    PIET offers various scholarships scheme for bright students like TFWS, SHANTI DEVI MEMORIAL LAPTOP SCHEME and many more.

  • HOW CAN I REACH THE PIET CAMPUSES ?
    •

    PIET Campuses are situated in Jaipur. The capital of Rajasthan. It is well connected by Road, Rail and Air.

    By Air
    It is 8 km away from Sanganer International Airport. Local Buses and Auto Rickshaw are available from International Airport.

    BY ROAD
    It is 20 km away from Jaipur bus stand (Sindhi Camp). Low Floor Buses and Auto Rickshaw are available from there.

    BY RAIL
    It is 25 km away from Jaipur Junction. Low Floor Buses and Auto Rickshaw are available from there.

  • IS THERE ANY FACILITY FOR PAYMENT OF HOSTEL FEE IN INSTALLMENT ?
    •

    Yes First Year Hostel Fees is to be paid in two installments.

  • IS THERE ANY AGENT WHO CAN HELP ME GETTING ADMISSION IN PIET ?
    •

    No, we don’t have any agents or middlemen for admissions. Admission process takes place in admission cell of PIET only.
